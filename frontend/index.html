Three lenses. Zero collusion.

Each persona only ever sees the same raw evidence packet — never another persona's score or reasoning. That isolation is what makes agreement mean something.

CODE & AUDIT HISTORY

The Auditor

Weighs audit coverage, unpatched findings, admin-key risk, and time-in-production — nothing else. Says "insufficient evidence" when the audit trail is thin, rather than guessing safe.

TVL & LIQUIDITY DEPTH

The Liquidity Analyst

Reads TVL trend, whale concentration, and withdrawal velocity. Flags thin or fleeing liquidity even on a protocol with a spotless audit.

EXPLOIT PATTERN MATCH

The Historian

Compares architecture and fork lineage against known exploits. Catches structural risk the other two personas structurally never look at.
